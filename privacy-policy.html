Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mobile application, Truck Monkey, designed for Amazon Relay drivers and dispatchers to track and manage delivery loads more efficiently.

1. Information Collection

Truck Monkey does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information. We do not require users to create accounts, submit personal details, or enable tracking permissions.

2. Device Access

The app may access location or notification services if explicitly permitted by the user for functionality, such as load updates or delivery alerts. These permissions are optional and managed entirely by your device settings.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not share your data with any third parties. No analytics, advertising, or third-party SDKs are used to track or profile users.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes to the app or regulatory requirements. You will be notified of any changes within the app or via the App Store listing.

5. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at truckmonkeyapp@gmail.com.
